About Our Mission

Your partner for
African expansion

YMM is an AFRICA-based market expansion services company, primarily in sub-Saharan Africa. We help companies who are looking for a reliable outsourcing partner to grow their business in new or existing markets. YMM is an African market expansion services provider with a strong focus on Africans countries who offer most fast growing opportunities. We help companies to develop their businesses in and across African countries. YMM offers a complete set of services that includes the organization and management of the value chain for any type of product or service. YMM provides its business partners with solid on-the-ground expertise, facilitating negotiations with governments and agencies, creating business bridges and tailor-made services based on a large, one-size-fits-all network and attractive depth.
