Raw Materials

Raw Materials & Innovative Ingredients

Our expertise covers the identification, sourcing, and valorization of unique African raw materials, with a particular focus on traceability and natural ingredients with high commercial potential for local and international markets.

Valorization of Unique African Resources

We support companies in identifying and exploiting the unique raw materials abundant in the African continent. Our approach combines on-the-ground knowledge, traceability expertise, and understanding of international markets.

From agroforestry to strategic minerals and high-value-added natural ingredients, we create sustainable value chains that benefit local communities while meeting the requirements of global markets.

Resource Identification

Detection of raw materials with high potential across the continent

Guaranteed Traceability

Comprehensive traceability systems from origin to processing

Optimal Valorization

Maximizing added value through innovative processes

Market Access

Connection with international buyers and processors

Our pan-African network allows us to operate in diverse sectors: non-timber forest products, medicinal plants, critical minerals, natural food ingredients. Each project is approached with a tailored method respectful of local ecosystems.
